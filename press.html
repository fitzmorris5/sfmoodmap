SF Mood Map — Press Kit

About

SF Mood Map turns San Francisco’s 311 reports into a live portrait of the city’s mood — block by block. It’s 100% public data, refreshed continuously, and designed for quick, sharable storytelling.

How it works

We classify recent 311 cases (last 6–48 hours) into five moods using transparent keyword rules, then color each neighborhood by its dominant mood. Hover any area for a breakdown and recent items. No accounts, no cookies.

Ethics & Privacy

Only public 311 data is used. We do not track users or collect personal data. The app is client-side and open by default.
